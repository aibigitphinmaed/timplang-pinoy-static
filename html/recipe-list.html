Breakfast

Corn Beef

A savory and tender salt-cured beef, packed with rich flavor and best enjoyed with rice.

Corn Beef
Ingredients:

1. 1 can corned beef
2. 1 small onion, chopped
3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
4. 1 small potato, diced (optional)
5. 1 tbsp cooking oil
6. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
2. Add diced potatoes and cook until slightly tender.
3. Stir in corned beef and cook for a few minutes.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Serve hot with rice and enjoy!

Tortilla Wrap

A delicious and versatile breakfast wrap filled with scrambled eggs, cheese, and your choice of meat or vegetables.

Tortilla Wrap
Ingredients:

1. 2 large tortillas
2. 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
3. 1/2 cup lettuce, chopped
4. 1/4 cup tomatoes, diced
5. 1/4 cup cheese, shredded
6. 2 tbsp mayonnaise or preferred dressing
Procedure:

1. Lay the tortillas flat on a clean surface.
2. Spread mayonnaise or dressing evenly over each tortilla.
3. Layer shredded chicken, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese on top.
4. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward and roll it tightly.
5. Slice in half and serve immediately.

Ampalaya

A healthy and hearty breakfast featuring sautéed bitter gourd (ampalaya) with eggs, offering a balanced mix of slight bitterness and savory goodness.

Ampalaya
Ingredients:

1. 1 medium ampalaya, sliced
2. 2 eggs, beaten
3. 1 small onion, chopped
4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
5. 1 tbsp cooking oil
6. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
2. Add sliced ampalaya and stir-fry for a few minutes.
3. Pour in beaten eggs and cook until set.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve hot with rice.

Omelette

A savory egg dish cooked in a pan and often filled with cheese, vegetables, or meat.

Omelette
Ingredients:

1. 2 eggs, beaten
2. 1 tbsp milk (optional)
3. 1 tbsp butter or oil
4. Fillings (cheese, ham, onions, bell peppers, etc.)
5. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Heat butter/oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Pour in the beaten eggs and cook until they start to set.
3. Add desired fillings and fold the omelette in half.
4. Cook for another minute, then serve hot.

Tapsilog

A classic Filipino breakfast dish featuring marinated beef (tapa), garlic rice (sinangag), and fried egg (itlog).

Tapsilog
Ingredients:

1. 200g beef sirloin, thinly sliced
2. 2 cloves garlic, minced
3. 1 tbsp soy sauce
4. 1 tbsp vinegar
5. 1 tbsp sugar
6. 1 cup cooked garlic rice
7. 1 egg, fried
8. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Marinate beef slices in garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper for at least 30 minutes.
2. Fry marinated beef until browned and cooked through.
3. Serve with garlic rice and fried egg.

Pan Cake

A soft, fluffy, and golden-brown flat cake made from a simple batter, often served with syrup, butter, or fruits.

Pan Cake
Ingredients:

1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
2. 1 tbsp sugar
3. 1 tsp baking powder
4. 1/2 tsp salt
5. 1 cup milk
6. 1 egg
7. 2 tbsp melted butter
Procedure:

1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just mixed.
4. Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter.
5. Pour batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form.
6. Flip and cook until golden brown.
7. Serve with syrup, butter, or fruits.

Soup

Arroz Caldo

A comforting Filipino rice porridge cooked with chicken, ginger, and garlic, perfect for a warm and hearty meal.

Arroz Caldo
Ingredients:

1. 1 cup glutinous rice
2. 1/2 kg chicken, cut into pieces
3. 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
4. 3 cloves garlic, minced
5. 1 small onion, chopped
6. 5 cups chicken broth
7. 1 tbsp fish sauce
8. Salt and pepper to taste
9. Hard-boiled egg (optional)
10. Chopped green onions and fried garlic for garnish
Procedure:

1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
3. Stir in glutinous rice and pour in chicken broth.
4. Let simmer until the rice is fully cooked and the mixture thickens.
5. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
6. Serve hot, garnished with boiled egg, fried garlic, and green onions.

Mushroom Soup

A creamy and delicious soup made from fresh mushrooms, milk, and seasonings, perfect for a warm and comforting dish.

Mushroom Soup
Ingredients:

1. 200g fresh mushrooms, sliced
2. 2 cups milk
3. 2 tbsp butter
4. 1 small onion, chopped
5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
6. 2 cups chicken broth
7. 1 tbsp flour
8. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Melt butter in a pot and sauté onion and garlic until soft.
2. Add mushrooms and cook until tender.
3. Sprinkle flour and mix well, then pour in chicken broth.
4. Simmer for 10 minutes, then add milk and stir continuously.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve hot.

Sopas

A Filipino-style creamy macaroni soup with chicken, milk, and vegetables, offering a warm and nourishing meal.

Sopas
Ingredients:

1. 200g elbow macaroni
2. 1 cup shredded chicken
3. 2 cups milk
4. 1 small onion, chopped
5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
6. 3 cups chicken broth
7. 1 carrot, diced
8. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Boil macaroni until al dente, then drain.
2. In a pot, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
3. Add chicken and cook for a few minutes.
4. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
5. Add cooked macaroni and carrots, then simmer.
6. Pour in milk, stir well, and season with salt and pepper.
7. Serve hot.

Squash Soup

A smooth and velvety soup made from blended squash, often seasoned with spices and cream for a rich flavor.

Squash Soup
Ingredients:

1. 2 cups squash, peeled and diced
2. 1 small onion, chopped
3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
4. 3 cups vegetable broth
5. 1/2 cup cream or evaporated milk
6. 1 tbsp butter
7. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Heat butter in a pot and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
2. Add squash and cook for a few minutes.
3. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
4. Simmer until the squash is tender.
5. Blend the mixture until smooth.
6. Stir in cream, season with salt and pepper, and serve warm.

Lunch

Bulalo

A rich and flavorful Filipino beef shank soup with bone marrow, slow-cooked to perfection.

Bulalo
Ingredients:

1. 1 kg beef shank with bone marrow
2. 1 liter water
3. 1 onion, quartered
4. 2 corn on the cob, cut into pieces
5. 1 bunch pechay or cabbage
6. 2 potatoes, quartered
7. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. In a pot, boil beef shank with water until tender, skimming off impurities.
2. Add onion and corn, simmer for 30 minutes.
3. Add potatoes and cook until tender.
4. Season with salt and pepper, then add pechay or cabbage.
5. Serve hot with rice.

Caldereta

A hearty and spicy Filipino stew made with tender beef, tomatoes, liver spread, and vegetables.

Caldereta
Ingredients:

1. 1 kg beef, cubed
2. 1 can liver spread
3. 2 cups tomato sauce
4. 1 cup potatoes, cubed
5. 1 cup carrots, cubed
6. 1 red bell pepper, sliced
7. 1 green bell pepper, sliced
8. 1 onion, chopped
9. 3 cloves garlic, minced
10. 2 cups water
11. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. In a pot, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
2. Add beef and brown on all sides.
3. Pour in tomato sauce and water, simmer until beef is tender.
4. Add liver spread, potatoes, and carrots, cook until soft.
5. Stir in bell peppers, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 5 minutes.
6. Serve hot with rice.

Kare-Kare

A traditional peanut stew featuring oxtail, vegetables, and a rich, creamy sauce.

Kare-Kare
Ingredients:

1. 1 kg oxtail, cut into pieces
2. 2 cups peanut butter
3. 1/2 cup annatto water
4. 1 eggplant, sliced
5. 1 bunch string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
6. 1 banana blossom, sliced
7. 3 cloves garlic, minced
8. 1 onion, chopped
9. 2 tbsp fish sauce
10. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Boil oxtail until tender, then set aside.
2. In a pan, sauté garlic and onion, then add annatto water.
3. Stir in peanut butter and mix well.
4. Add boiled oxtail, then pour in stock from the boiled meat.
5. Add vegetables and simmer until cooked.
6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
7. Serve with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side.

Menudo

Sinigang

A sour and savory Filipino soup made with pork or shrimp, tamarind broth, and fresh vegetables.

Sinigang
Ingredients:

1. 1 kg pork ribs or shrimp
2. 1 packet tamarind soup mix or fresh tamarind
3. 1 liter water
4. 2 tomatoes, quartered
5. 1 onion, quartered
6. 1 cup radish, sliced
7. 1 cup string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
8. 1 eggplant, sliced
9. 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
10. Salt and fish sauce to taste
Procedure:

1. Boil pork ribs or shrimp in water with onion and tomatoes.
2. Add tamarind soup mix or fresh tamarind extract.
3. Add radish, string beans, and eggplant, and cook until vegetables are tender.
4. Season with fish sauce and salt to taste.
5. Add kangkong leaves and let it simmer for another minute.
6. Serve hot with rice.

Bicol Express

A spicy and creamy Filipino dish made with pork, coconut milk, chilies, and shrimp paste.

Bicol Express
Ingredients:

1. 500g pork belly, sliced
2. 2 cups coconut milk
3. 1 cup coconut cream
4. 5-6 red chilies, sliced
5. 3 cloves garlic, minced
6. 1 onion, chopped
7. 2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
8. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. In a pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
2. Add pork and cook until lightly browned.
3. Stir in shrimp paste and cook for a few minutes.
4. Pour in coconut milk and let it simmer until pork is tender.
5. Add chilies and coconut cream, cook until sauce thickens.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Serve hot with rice.

Baked Scallops

Deliciously baked scallops topped with garlic butter and cheese for a rich, savory flavor.

Baked Scallops
Ingredients:

1. 12 scallops
2. 2 tbsp butter, melted
3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
4. 1/4 cup cheese, grated
5. 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
6. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Place scallops on a baking tray.
3. Mix butter, garlic, salt, and pepper, then drizzle over scallops.
4. Top with grated cheese and bake for 15 minutes.
5. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.

Pusit

Grilled Pusit
Ingredients:

1. 500g fresh squid, cleaned
2. 2 tbsp soy sauce
3. 1 tbsp calamansi juice
4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
5. 1 tbsp cooking oil
6. Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:

1. Marinate squid with soy sauce, calamansi juice, garlic, salt, and pepper for 20 minutes.

Dessert

Bibingka

A traditional Filipino rice cake made with coconut milk, rice flour, and sugar, often topped with salted egg and cheese.

Bibingka
Ingredients:

1. 1 cup rice flour
2. 1 cup all-purpose flour
3. 1 cup sugar
4. 1 cup coconut milk
5. 1 tsp baking powder
6. 1/2 tsp salt
7. 2 eggs
8. Salted eggs, sliced (for topping)
9. Cheese, grated (for topping)
Procedure:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a bowl, combine rice flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
3. Add coconut milk and eggs, and mix well.
4. Pour batter into greased baking pans.
5. Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until golden brown.
6. Top with salted eggs and cheese before serving.

Puto Bumbong

Tupig

Snacks

Balut

A famous Filipino delicacy of boiled fertilized duck egg, enjoyed with salt or vinegar.

Balut
Ingredients:

1. 6 fertilized duck eggs
2. Salt or vinegar for dipping
Procedure:

Binagol

A sweet and sticky taro-based delicacy wrapped in banana leaves.

Binagol
Ingredients:

1. 2 cups taro, boiled and mashed
2. 1 cup sugar
3. 1/2 cup coconut milk
4. Banana leaves for wrapping
Procedure:

1. Mix mashed taro, sugar, and coconut milk until smooth.
2. Spoon the mixture into banana leaves and fold to seal.
3. Steam for about 30-40 minutes until firm.
4. Let cool before serving.

Champorado

A comforting chocolate rice porridge served with milk, perfect for rainy days.

Champorado
Ingredients:

1. 1 cup glutinous rice
2. 1/2 cup cocoa powder
3. 4 cups water
4. Milk and sugar to taste
Procedure:

1. Rinse the glutinous rice and boil it in water until soft.
2. Stir in cocoa powder and mix until well combined.
3. Serve hot with milk and sugar.

Potato Croquettes

Crispy on the outside and creamy on the inside, these delicious potato croquettes make a perfect snack or side dish.

Potato Croquettes
Ingredients:

1. 2 cups mashed potatoes
2. 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
3. 1/4 cup grated cheese
4. Salt and pepper to taste
5. Oil for frying
Procedure:

1. Mix mashed potatoes, breadcrumbs, cheese, salt, and pepper.
2. Form into small balls or patties.

Strawberry Taho

A sweet and refreshing snack made with silken tofu, arnibal (sweet syrup), and fresh strawberries.

Strawberry Taho
Ingredients:

1. 1 cup silken tofu
2. 1/2 cup arnibal (sweet syrup)
3. 1/2 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
4. Sago pearls (optional)
Procedure:

1. Gently heat the silken tofu until warm.
2. Drizzle arnibal over the tofu.
3. Top with fresh strawberries and sago pearls.
4. Serve warm or chilled.

Tusok-Tusok

A street food term referring to skewered fish balls, kikiam, and squid balls fried to perfection.

Tusok-Tusok
Ingredients:

1. 1 cup fish balls
2. 1 cup kikiam (meat rolls)
3. 1 cup squid balls
4. Oil for frying
5. Sweet and spicy sauce for dipping
Procedure:

1. Skewer fish balls, kikiam, and squid balls on sticks.
2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the skewers until golden brown.
3. Serve with sweet and spicy sauce for dipping.
