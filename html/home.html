Adobong Manok

Kare-Kare

A peanut-based stew with oxtail, tripe, and vegetables, often paired with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste).

Halo-Halo

A colorful dessert made of crushed ice, milk, sweetened beans, fruits, jelly, and topped with leche flan or ube ice cream.

Pancit

Stir-fried noodles with vegetables, meat, or seafood, often served during celebrations as a symbol of longevity and prosperity.

Sinigang na Baboy

A sour pork soup with vegetables like kangkong, radish, and eggplant, loved for its tangy flavor.
