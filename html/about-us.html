Who we are

Timplang Pinoy is all about sharing the rich and flavorful world of Filipino
cuisine in a way that's easy and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're
a beginner in the kitchen or a seasoned cook, we provide simple, reliable
recipes and cooking tips to help you recreate your favorite Filipino dishes
at home.

What started as a passion for preserving and celebrating traditional Filipino
flavors has grown into a trusted space for home cooks looking for inspiration.
Our team is made up of food lovers, recipe creators, and storytellers who
are dedicated to keeping the heart of Filipino cooking alive. Join us in bringing
the warmth and joy of Filipino food to your table.

Recipe Development

Our recipes are developed and tested by our team of experienced home cooks
and food professionals to ensure that they are easy to follow, reliable, and
delicious. We take pride in creating recipes that capture the essence of Filipino
cuisine while making them accessible to everyone. Whether you're looking
for a classic adobo recipe or a modern twist on a traditional dish, we've got
you covered. Our goal is to help you explore the diverse and vibrant flavors of
Filipino cooking and inspire you to get creative in the kitchen.

Inclusion

At Timplang Pinoy, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to
experience the joy of Filipino food. That's why we strive to create a welcoming
and inclusive space for all home cooks, regardless of their background or
experience. Whether you're a Filipino living abroad, a non-Filipino looking
to explore a new cuisine, or someone who simply loves good food, we
welcome you with open arms. Our recipes are designed to be approachable
and adaptable, so you can feel confident in the kitchen no matter your
skill level. We're here to help you discover the beauty and diversity of
Filipino cuisine and make it a part of your culinary journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate and share the rich culinary heritage of the
Philippines with the world. We are committed to preserving traditional
Filipino flavors and recipes while also embracing innovation and creativity
in the kitchen. By providing accessible and reliable recipes, cooking
tips, and resources, we aim to empower home cooks to explore the
vibrant world of Filipino cuisine and create memorable dining experiences
for themselves and their loved ones. We believe that food has the power
to bring people together, and we're dedicated to spreading the warmth and
joy of Filipino cooking to every corner of the globe.
